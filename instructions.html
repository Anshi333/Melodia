1. Press the button to begin the game.
  2. Watch as a colored button lights up and listen to its sound.
  3. Press the same button that lit up to repeat the sequence.
  4. After each correct response, a new button is added to the sequence.
  5. Repeat the entire sequence in the correct order.
  6. Continue until you make a mistake or reach the game's highest level.
  7. Press to go to home page.
